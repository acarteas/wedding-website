Viva Las Vegas

Hotel

The wedding party will be staying at the Tuscany Suites and Casino. The 108 Paradise - RTC bus can be taken directly from the airport to the hotel. Check your invitation for a discount code that can be used when booking your stay.

Ceremony Location

The ceremony will be held at The Wedding Chapel. The ceremony begins promptly at 12PM so be sure to arrive by 11:30AM. The same 108 Paradise – RTC bus can be taken from the hotel to the wedding chapel.

Reception

The reception will be held at Fogo De Chao, which is conveniently located across the street from our hotel. The reception runs from 2-5PM. Plan to arrive at around 1:30PM. Bring your appetite!
