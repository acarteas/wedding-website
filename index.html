Chia Adam

It all started with a turtle. Adam was browsing the dating site Ok Cupid and came across Chia’s profile. Thinking she was cute, Adam realized that she probably got quite a few messages from other men. He knew that he had to be clever and concise. Adam knew he had an “in” when he came across a photo of Chia swimming with a turtle. So, he typed up something clever and clicked send. At the time, Adam didn’t realize that Chia has a special connection to turtles. His message cut through the noise and he got his date.

They hit it off right away and after their first date at Phikun's Thai restaurant. Early on, Chia remembers quite fondly that as they lay in the wheatfields outside of her house and gaze up into the stars, Adam states “If nights were just like these forever, I would propose to you right now.” Well as any of you know, love is complicated and there are the good times and the hard times. Adam and Chia have had both. But love is in part a choice. We choose to love and we choose to commit to the ones we love.

After dating for about fifteen months, and living with each other for about nine months, Adam and Chia parted ways. Adam had just graduated Washington State University and he was offered a full-time teaching position at Humboldt State University. Prior to applying to HSU, Chia insisted that Adam apply to one school in CA because Chia wanted to move back to her home state. Low and behold, after applying to a handful of schools, Adam was offered a job in California.

Their relationship became rocky during the time surrounding his graduation in May of 2016 and Adam moved down to Eureka, CA without his Califonian sweetheart. Chia stayed in Pullman, WA, and completed her M.A. degree in Anthropology.

After about a year and a half apart, Chia and Adam started talking again. After a few months of talking to Adam on the phone and skyping, Chia took a leap of faith and moved to Eureka to be with Adam.

On 2’s 2/22/22, Adam and Chia will have known each other for seven years. They will have dated for 5 and a ½ years and they look forward to spending the next seventy years with each other.
